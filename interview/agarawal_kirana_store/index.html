Agarawal kirana store

BACKGROUND:

Shivnath Agarwal, aka Bablu aged 43 , rules the market of Tagore Garden shopping complex in West Delhi through his supreme quality kirana store. Agarwal Kirana store locally known as Bablu Ki Dukan by the residents is probably the destination for traditional quality kirana items ranging from spices to toiletries to packaged food and what not.

Bablu is a married man. He has his wife Shashi Agarwal, a son Shiv, aged 7 years and his father Om Prakash Agarwal. Bablu Kirana store is more like a family heritage for Bablu and is run by him and his wife at present.

Let’s take a sneak into his sore !

Q. WHY DID BABLU START HIS BUSINESS ?

Bablu was not the one to start his business. It was his father Om Prakash ji instead who had run the business for almost 15 years before he handed it over to Bablu. Although Bablu was a graduate in BA Programme , he always was inclined towards taking over the business and maintain the same level of customer trust and loyalty as his father.

Q. WHEN DID HE START HIS BUSINESS?

Bablu simply took over the business almost 12 years ago, that is 2008, however, his father started the business in 1994.

Q. WHAT DOES HIS BUSINESS DEAL WITH?

His business deals with all sorts of kirana items as well as consumer goods such as spices, condiments, flour, toiletries , packaged food etc.

Q. HOW DID HE MANAGE THE INITIAL CAPITAL?

Since it was his father who started the business, Bablu is aware of the fact that the initial capital was borrowed from multiple relatives along with the minimum savings of his grandfather. No formal loans were involved in the initial funding.

Q. WHAT WERE THE MAJOR DIFFICULTIES IN RUNNING THE BUSINESS?

The major difficulties which his father faced were establishing a permanent footing in the market complex among many other kirana stores, which he attained by sticking by to the quality of the products more than anything else. The major difficulty Bablu faces today is surviving among the most modern departmental stores which provide huge goods at discounted prices and offers. What Bablu follows to deal with it is exactly what his father did – never compromise on the quality !

Q. DOES HE GET HELP FROM HIS FAMILY MEMBERS?

Yes, Bablu not only gets help from his family members but his store is run by him and his wife along with his cousin brothers .

Q. HOW MANY SUPPORT HAS HE HIRED?

Bablu has hired only two outside supports, Yadav and Suresh who look after the home delivery of big orders. Apart from that he, his wife and two of his cousin brothers look after the shop.

Q. FOR THE MOST TIME IN SHOP, WHAT DOES HE DO?

Bablu spends almost 10 hours a day in his shop. For most of the time he is involved in dealing with ongoing customers at all times of the day, even afternoon. Apart from this he tallies his accounts, chit chats with his wife and brothers , shares pieces of information and gossips with the customers and if free for even longer any day , then lays his hands on a newspaper.

Q. WHAT ARE HIS TOP PRIORITIES?

As mentioned earlier, his top priority is maintaining the quality of his goods even if it calls for slightly higher prices than the usual rates, because that he believes will always retain the faith of the customers.

Q. WHO ARE THE TYPICAL CUSTOMERS?

The typical customers include the residents of the locality and the nearby areas.

Q. DOES HE USE A POS TERMINAL FOR ACCEPTING PAYMENTS? WHY OR WHY NOT?

No, he does not use a POS terminal for accepting payments for the simple reason that he only prefers one mode of payment which is Cash payment.

Q. WHAT IS THE PERCENTAGE OF CASH TRANSACTIONS AT HIS SHOP?

The percentage of cash transactions at his shop is 100, as he only deals in on the spot cash payments.

Q. WHAT IS THE PERCENTAGE OF QR CODE PAYMENTS?

The percentage of QR code payments is 0.

Q. DOES HE USE AN ATM? WHY DOES HE WITHDRAW MONEY FROM THE ATM?

Yes, he uses and ATM. He withdraws money from the ATM for attaining cash to buy his products from his wholesaler, clearing the payments of his staff and for his household expenses as well.

Q. HOW MUCH MONEY DOES HE MAKE IN A MONTH?

Bablu manages to make around 60000 to 70000 in a month at present.

Q. DOES HE TAKE LOAN FROM EXTERNAL PEOPLE FOR RUNNING HIS SHOP?

No, Bablu says he luckily never had to take external loans to run his shop as even at the low times , he managed to suffice with the savings .

Q. HAS HE REGISTERED FOR GST?

Yes, he is in the final procedure for registering for GST.

Q. HOW MUCH DOES HE PAY MONTHLY FOR ELECTRICITY, PHONE, GAS AND SHOP RENT?

He pays Rs 2000 / month for electricity, Rs 2500/ month for phone. There is no gas expense at the shop and he owns the area thus no shop rent either.

Q. WHAT ARE THE APPS HE USES TO RUN HIS BUSINESS?

Bablu still relies on the traditional business methods and uses only Whatsapp for running his business.

Q. WHAT APPS DOES HE USE OTHERWISE?

He uses Whatsapp and facebook only.

Q. WHAT DOES HE DO IN HIS FREE TIME?

In his free time Bablu manages to go home and spend time with his son or watch television.
