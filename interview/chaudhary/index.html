Chaudhary stores

This is about Nirav of age 50. He has a small store of groceries named as chaudhary stores in Surat. He started business to live a better life. He has a family of five, He has two children, wife and mother.

From two children,one is girl studying 11th science and her brother is BBA graduate currently serving as an employee in one of the company.

He started his business in 2012.His store have groceries at a comparatively low price with assured quality and quantity so he always have 2-3 waiting customers at his shop.

From his provident fund which he got from the company,he invested to start this grocery store.

He faced many difficulties initially like not getting a single customer in a day but he didn’t give up. Instead of giving up,he started working harder. His family always supported him even he wasn’t earning much initially.

When he was out in delievery of groceries, his wife or mother takes care of the store as their home to store distance wasn’t much more. This made him more stable for running the store. Now he had placed one supporter in his store.

In most of the time in the store he plays music or completes his pending works of the store. His top priorities are his family and his business.

Typical customers bargains or are short temper. He uses POS terminal as his responsibility. Percentage transaction of his shop is around 5000 per day. QR code transaction is around 3000 per day. He uses ATM to withdraw money for monthly household expenses which he had credited from his profit.

He has a monthly income of around 25,000.

He hadn’t taken loan as all investment was managed from provident fund.

He had registered for GST.

His monthly expense of electricity, phone, gas is around 5000. Getting updated with technology, he uses whatsApp to run his shop. He can also use Facebook to run his store by advertising and communicating with more people.

He spends his free time with his family by picnicking,camping,playing games,etc.
