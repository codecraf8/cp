Devender

Short Story

Devender Thakur, age 28 runs a general store in Delhi. He lives with his parents, two younger sisters, younger brother and wife. Devender’s two younger sisters and brother work in their respective offices and help him at the shop as well. His dad also helps him out financially whenever required and his mother is a homemaker. He started the business 9 years back as he was not satisfied with his job. The story dives into how a normal college student became a really successful businessman.

At his store, he deals with all kinds of general items and he ensures that the customers don’t return unsatisfied from his store. The business is run with the help of family members and is very successful in today’s date. He neither is a part of any trader’s association nor has any tie-ups with brands.

There is no peak season for his business as it is a general store but the peak timings at the store are 7a.m. to 11a.m. and 6p.m. to 9p.m.

QNA WITH DEVENDER THAKUR

Q. What is the name of the merchant and his age?

A. Name of merchant is Devender Thakur and his age is 28.

Q. Name of the shop and locality?

A. Name of the shop is N.H. General Store and it is located very close to the surrounding colonies.

Q. Why did he start his business?

A. Devender decided to start his business after he didn’t get any satisfaction from his job where he had been work for around 1½ years.

Q. Who are the members in his family?

A. Devender’s family includes his parents, his two younger sisters, a younger brother and his wife.

Q. When did he start his business?

A. He started his business back in 2011.

Q. What does his business deal with?

A. His business deals with all kinds of general items which are required in day to day life along with stationary items and photocopying documents.

Q. How did he manage with the initial capital?

A. The thing that Devender is proud of till date is that he never had to take any loans from anywhere and his father always supported him financially.

Q. What were the major difficulties in running his business?

A. He never faced any major difficulty in his business as he was talented and always knew how to attract more and more customers to his shop which is why he got both the interior and exterior of his shop upgraded and his shop looked distinctively different and better than other shops. He knew that good presentation skills always work and that’s what worked out. All the competition from the surrounding shops got cleared and his soon became the best seller of the locality.

Q. Does he get help from his family members?

A. He does get help from his family members, in fact his family help him out a lot whenever they get time.

Q. How many support has he hired?

A. Because of extensive support by family, he never felt the need of hiring someone else.

Q. For the most of time he is in shop what does he do?

A. When in shop, he stays busy with the customers and when free, he plays mobile games.

Q. What are his top priorities?

A. His top priority is customer satisfaction no matter what.

Q. Who are his typical customers?

A. His typical customers include people from the neighbouring societies who take regular visits at his store and rely upon him completely.

Q. Does he use a POS Terminal for accepting payments, if yes, why, if no, why not?

A. Yes, he uses PhonePe, PayTM, Amazon Pay, other UPI apps and all other types of electronic payment methods by QR Code as well.

Q. What is the %age of cash transaction at his shop?

A. 65% of transactions at his shop are done with cash.

Q. What is the %age of QR code payments at his shop?

A. 35% of his transactions at his shop are done with QR code.

Q. Does he use ATM? Why does he withdraw money from the ATM?

A. He never feels the need of visiting bank or ATM. According to him, the last time he visited an ATM it was 7 to 8 months back.

Q. How much money does he make in a month?

A. On an average he is able to earn 50,000 to 60,000 rupees per month.

Q. Does he take loan from external people for running his shop? If yes, what is the interest he pays.

A. No.

Q. How does he re-pay his loan - UPI / Cash / Cheque / Online?

A. He never felt the need to take a loan.

Q. Has he registered for GST?

A. No.

Q. How much does he pay monthly for electricity, phone, gas, shop-rent?

A. Monthly, including phone, gas and other expenses he has to spend around 6,000 rupees per month.

Q. What are the apps he uses for running his business?

A. For running his business, he uses apps like KhataBook and WhatsApp.

Q. What apps does he otherwise use?

A. Other than his business, he uses all social media apps and Call Of Duty: Mobile and Need for Speed.

Q. What does he do in his free time?

A. In his free time, he does what he loves to do, that is picking up his bike and going for a long drive.
