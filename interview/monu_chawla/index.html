Monu Chawla

Story

Monu Chawla (28) runs a general goods store in Delhi. He lives with his mother, father, brother and his wife in Delhi,Shahdara. This business was started by his father when he was 10 or so. In beginning this shop was not doing well, they had to struggle really hard for years. After 5-6 year there struggle paid off and they start growing with quite good customer base. After few years monu’s elder brother (Mohit) completed his CA and guided them to expand their business. Then they started to expand among more categories like Milk Products, Frozen Foods, etc. After 2 years after completing his BBA, Monu joined his father at the age of 24. Now their store is quite big and known as a landmark in their locality.

Asks and Replies

Business

The store usually sells all kind of daily day to day life products like soaps, snacks, milk, etc but during occasions they adds gift items like chocolates, Packed Juices, etc. The store caters to nearly whole locality of more than 50 Houses and they also provides home delivery with neglegible charges. I am not associated with any association or brand. It’s his family business. His father started this business in terrible conditions with time things starts getting better and after cpmpleting my BBA i joined my father and now i runs this business. It’s quite hard to tell about initial investment but it was like Rs 70,000.

Q. How you accept orders?

Customers usually text us their orders via whatsapp or Calls us where our staff replies and delivers their goods. We accepts orders from 10 am to 2 pm then all the goods will be delivered till 7pm. Will you try to bring your business online? Yes, we will definitely try to bring our business online in upcoming years but currently we are focusing on expanding our business by opening more branches across delhi then we’ll all try cross chain delivery.

Medium of transaction

Usually customers prefers cash over online payment platforms like PayTM, PhonePe, etc but they supports all kind of payment methods. Due corona virus pandemic they saw 30% rise in online transaction and home deliveries.

Expansion Plans

Our team is continuously working on expanding among multiple categories and planning to open a branch very soon. Currently their priority is to provide best customer service.

Loans

Usually we doesn’t need any loan to expand our busniess, we just reinvests 70% of whatever they earns. As i told we are planning to expanding our business so we might take Rs 25 Lakh loan at minimum interest as possible.

Employees

The store currently holds 3 boys. One for delivery and two for sales and management.

Taxation and ITRs

Taxation and ITRs are quite smooth as my brother is CA and he handles all these stuffs.

Expenses

We pays approximately Rs 50,000 per month to our staff+incentives and Rs 20,000 for other expenses like electricity, maintenance, etc.

Tech Adoption

As I told, we upgades ourself with time. We accepts all kind of digital payment, computers for data handling,customer support and billing. Profit and Annual revenue Our current annual revenue is like 60-70 lakh per annum from whom we makes a profit of approximately 20 lakh per annnum.

Business Goal

Our Business Goal is to provide best customer support with valid price. We are continously adopting and adding new goods and stuffs to our business which makes us unique from other local businesses. We always appreciates heathy competition as it helps us to evolves with time.
