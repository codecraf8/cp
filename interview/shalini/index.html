Name of the kirana shop is “Sridevi kirana and General stores” and the locality of that shop is pm palem Doctor varma complex plot no 241, Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh.

Q. Why did he start his business?

She is the only daughter of the family and the shop was handed over to her by her father. She has been running the shop ever since.

Q. Who are the members in his family?

She lives with her husband, daughter,son and a small pet in a rented house.

Q. When did he start his business?

She started her business long ago when she completed her intermediate.

Q. What does his business deal with?

Her business deals with all the groceries and other sundries like Rice, Dal, Flours (Wheat Flour, Rice Flour, etc.),Sugar/Jaggery, Pices (Khada Masalas & Powdered Masalas), Vegetables (Onions & Potatoes), Tea/Coffee, Snacks and many more.

She managed with the help of his ancestors properties and for developing it she took help from a bank which provided loans.

  1. - At times they face challenges like hiring employees whereas when the employee takes leave and in her(employee) absence she faces challenges because she cannot continue to manage both her business hours and personal hours.

  2. - Yes, she says that she cannot spend some summer time with their family going out for a vacation.

  3. - And also, there are ups and downs in the business when they get low profit when compared to the peak seasons. So, at these times like in pandemic season “She says that, they avoid taking any breaktime away from work.”

She said her family is Bank. With a small smile on her face, she said there is no one to help me from my family members side. Because as theirs was love marriage no one supported her once she got married.

She hired 1 girl named Kalpana, with the help of her servant who works in her home and that girl is an orphan who works part time for her living. She has been working in that store for the past 4 years.

She said that she is too busy everyday handling things at the shop by contacting the traders and many more. She says it is not just the different types of local products they sell.She says that she usually provides free delivery in less than an hour for regular customers.

She laughed aloud and replied “I don’t know hindi and bengali language and most of the customers are bengalis and they either speak in hindi nor bengali where I cannot match with them” this will be the hectic phase I face everyday and at this time customers think that they are not getting proper service from our end.
