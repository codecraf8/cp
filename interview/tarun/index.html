Tarun

- Tarun Aggarwal, 37 , is a owner of a grocery store in Delhi ( Chanakya Puri). He lives in Kalka ji with his wife and 2 daughters, Priya(15) and Aarti(12) who studies in a Government school. Tarun’s elder brother Akhil Aggarwal,42, runs a electrical appliances shop in Malviya Nagar and lives with his family in Munirka.

- Tarun’s father started the business in 2001 in a shop on rent but now Tarun owns that shop. He took over the business in 2011. He believes that the shop is the love and blessings from his father. Akhil wanted to do something big ,but later he ended up with the electronic appliances shop.

- As a retailer, he faces some problems because now- a- days people prefer to buy products online. Even he is planning to take the business online through various online platforms. The shop named ‘Aggarwal Store’ sells all grocery related products and some stationary items too. The main problem for him is that there are many grocery shops in that area, which increases the competition.

- The store provides home delivery services also with the help of his two employees Nitiesh and Rahul. They both belong to the same family. Tarun also provides pick and drop facilities to his helpers. They are working for over 5 years in this shop.

- He doesn’t have any tie ups with any brand. There is no special season for a grocery store so the drill remains the same throughout the year. The timings for the store are 11.00 am- 9.00 pm( including weekends).

- Payment applications like Pay tm, Google pay, Phone Pe have eased the problems of making or receiving a payment.

- The owner believes that ' Customers are God' and they deserve a right bill. For this, a billing machine is available at the store which also helps in keeping the track of all the transactions or expenses.

- Most of the transactions takes place in submitting the electricity bills and the employees salary. He pays them through cash.

- After all the payments are done, he earns about 35,000-40,000 per month.

- Various kinds of customers visit the store every. From those sophisticated class to the labours.

- He doesn’t distinguish between the customers and treat them with utmost respect. Cash, UPI transactions, Google pay, phone pe, Pay tm are the major modes of payment available for the customers. About 40 percent of the customer pay through these apps and the rest prefer cash payment. He makes sure that he is able to meet his customer’s need. He attends approximately 40-45 customers on a average daily basis. Among them 10-15 are his daily customers. Mainly, the customers are from the societies nearby.

- Tarun makes the best use of technology to ease out the problems.

- While his free time in the shop he spends his time on applications like WhatsApp, Facebook, YouTube , playing games, or talking to his family in the lunch time.

- His life aim is to extend the business as much as he can and that his daughters study hard and write a bright future for themselves.
